Sorry for the love

Sorry for the love
which makes me cure,
sorry for the love
which you feel insecure.

Sorry for the love
which lightened me,
Sorry for the love
which frightened you.

I feels it, like following
you feels it, like stalking.

How insane I was
thinking, your smile is cause of love
by seeing that, I blush.

that sparks the power of love
then you are in my heart
you are in my dream
by thinking you my day pass.

Day by day that spark grows
killing the negativity, stuffing the positivity
healing the wounds
curing the hurts
growing it's territory
to capture the heart.

Your gaze powered me
motivate me.
world is new place to me
cherish every moment of my life.

You capture my heart
you capture my soul
but you never love me as a whole.

You were not here
my eyes tries to find you everywhere
sometimes hands sketches your face
eyes complete it in any case.

Love me not, but talk to me
Your silence hurts me

I was never with you
never walk with you
never talk with you
but there are some moments of us
which makes me happy
filled my eyes
whenever I remembered them
makes me full of joyous.

Sorry for the love
which makes me cure,
Sorry for the love
which you feel insecure.

Sorry for the love
which lightened me,
Sorry for the love
which frightened you.

I know it's not love
cause I heard,"Love happened from both sides"
we are not together
but whatever it is,
it's forever.
