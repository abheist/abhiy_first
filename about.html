HEY

I'm Abhishek Kumar Singh

22 year old, UX Designer & Developer

From

Agra, India

But I've lived in various other places.

Studying

Computer Science & Engineering

But I'm always curious about things I don't know yet.

Plays

Table Tennis

But I'm mostly up for any kind of sport.

Loves to

Draw

Always doodling, kind of habit.

Some books that shaped my thinking.

First, let me just admit it. I've always been doing things people around me found crazy. I believe that I can do anything I truly believe in.

"Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve."

I began kicking around pixels and code back in 2012. My journey started from an highly deep urge of making a website, self-taught myself and start making my own website and taking more & more projects from others for free. Soon enough I realized that this is just the phase of learning a bunch of tools and skills which I focused on and started improving with time.

"Experience is simply the name we give our mistakes."

After that I started thinking that there is much more I can do, which can improve lives of other people, in so many ways. And there marked the beginning of product thinking. I started making things that, I believed, can simplify people's lives, solve their problems. Which is exactly what I'm trying to do now.

Solve the problems that solve the problem.

I like listening to people's ideas and helping bring them to life. If you've got any potential idea that could help others grow, let's have a chat sometime.

And thank you for taking time to read about me. :)
