HEY

I'm Abhishek Kumar Singh

22 year old, UX Designer & Developer

From

Agra, India

But I've lived in various other places.

Studying

Computer Science & Engineering

But I'm always curious about things I don't know yet.

Plays

Table Tennis

But I'm mostly up for any kind of sport.

Loves to

Draw

Always doodling, kind of habit.

Some books that shaped my thinking.

First, let me just admit it that I do things what people found crazy enough and in there deepdown there is a highly deep urge to change the world and make it a better place.

"The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do"

Since my childhood I have a keen interest in the artistic things and when I was in class 2nd my brother bought a first computer and left me crazy what whole lot of things I can do with it. If I go back and connect the dots then these all things happen in a sequence. Since then my interest in computer arises and I'm always at my best when you talk about computers. And then finally I chose computer as my career.

"Remembering that you are going to die is the best way I know to avoid the trap of thinking you have something to lose. You are already naked. There is no reason not to follow your heart."

I began tinkering around code since 2008 and with pixels from 2012. My journey started from an highly deep urge of making an amazing website, self-taught myself and start making my own website and taking more and more projects from others for free. Soon enough I realized that this is just the phase of learning a bunch of tools and skills which I focused on and started improving with time.

"Knowing is not enough; we must apply. Willing is not enough; we must do."

Then I started realising that there is much more I can do. I can improve the lives of others in many ways. Then creativity lies, I started making things I believe can simplify people's lives, solve their problem. Which is what exactly I'm trying to do now.

"Solve a problem that solve the problem"

I love to listen people's ideas and helping bring them to life. If you got any amazing idea that could help others grow, let's have a chat sometime.

And thank you for taking time to read about me. :)
